KM Joinery PWA Test

This page is designed to test the Progressive Web App (PWA) functionality of the KM Joinery Quote Generator.

How to Install on iPhone:

  1. Open this page in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (square with up arrow) at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Give the app a name (or keep the default) and tap "Add"
  5. The app will now appear on your home screen with its icon

How to Install on Mac:

  1. Open this page in Safari
  2. Click on "Safari" in the menu bar
  3. Select "Add to Dock..."
  4. Click "Add" in the dialog that appears
